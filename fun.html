Fun Commands

Commands for fun and simple interactions.

Default Prefix: .

.hello

Syntax: .hello

Aliases: .chào, .lô

A simple command to make the bot say hello back to you.

Examples:

.hello

The bot will respond with "Hello @YourUsername!".

.dice

Syntax: .dice [sides]

Rolls a virtual die.

Parameters:

  • [sides] (Optional): Number of sides on the die (Default: 6, Max: 10000).

Examples:

.dice
.dice 20

.rps

Syntax: .rps

Aliases: .rockpaperscissors

Play Rock Paper Scissors against the bot.

Parameters:

  • (Required): Your choice.

Examples:

.rps rock

.poll

Syntax: .poll "" "" "" ... ""

Creates a simple poll with up to 10 options using reactions.

Required Permissions:

  • Bot: Add Reactions, Send Messages, Embed Links, [Maybe Manage Messages to delete user command]
  • User: None

Parameters:

  • "" (Required): The poll question, enclosed in quotes.
  • "" "" ... (Required): At least two options, each enclosed in quotes (max 10 total).

Examples:

.poll "Favorite Color?" "Red" "Blue" "Green"

Note: Ensure options are enclosed in quotes if they contain spaces.
