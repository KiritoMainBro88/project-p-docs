Music Commands

Commands to play music in voice channels. Supports YouTube, SoundCloud, Spotify links, and search terms.

Default Prefix: . (Main command group is play or alias p)

.play

Syntax: .play

Aliases: .p

Plays a song or adds it to the queue. Joins your voice channel if not connected.

Required Permissions:

  • Bot: Connect, Speak, Embed Links
  • User: Must be in a voice channel.

Parameters:

  • (Required): A direct URL (YouTube, SoundCloud, Spotify track/album/playlist) or text to search for on YouTube.

Examples:

.play https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=...
.p Lo-fi Hip Hop Radio
.play https://open.spotify.com/track/...

Note: For Spotify playlists/albums, the bot typically searches for and plays the first track found based on the metadata.

.play stop

Syntax: .play stop

Aliases: .p stop, .p leave, .p disconnect, .p dc

Stops the music, clears the queue, and disconnects the bot from the voice channel.

Examples:

.p stop

.play skip

Syntax: .play skip

Aliases: .p skip, .p s, .p next

Skips the currently playing song and plays the next one in the queue.

Examples:

.p skip

.play queue

Syntax: .play queue [page]

Aliases: .p queue, .p q, .p list

Displays the current music queue.

Parameters:

  • [page] (Optional): The page number of the queue to view (Default: 1).

Examples:

.p queue
.p q 2

.play nowplaying

Syntax: .play nowplaying

Aliases: .p nowplaying, .p np

Shows detailed information about the currently playing song, including duration, progress, requester, volume, and loop status.

Examples:

.p np

.play volume

Syntax: .play volume [percentage]

Aliases: .p volume, .p vol

Shows or sets the music playback volume (0-200%).

Parameters:

  • [percentage] (Optional): Volume level from 0 to 200. If omitted, displays the current volume.

Examples:

.p volume
.p vol 80
.p vol 150

.play loop

Syntax: .play loop [off|song|queue]

Aliases: .p loop

Shows or sets the loop mode for the music player.

Parameters:

  • [off|song|queue] (Optional): The loop mode to set. 'song' repeats the current track, 'queue' repeats the entire queue. If omitted, displays the current mode.

Examples:

.p loop
.p loop song
.p loop queue
.p loop off

.play 24/7

Syntax: .play 24/7

Aliases: .p 24/7, .p stay, .p keep

Toggles whether the bot should stay connected to the voice channel even when the queue is empty.

Required Permissions:

  • User: Manage Guild

Examples:

.p 24/7
